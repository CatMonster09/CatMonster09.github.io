Imagine Dragons

Imagine Dragons is a rock band that consists of lead vocalist Dan Reynolds, bass player Ben Mckee, drummer Daniel Platzman, and lead guitarist Daniel Wayne Sermon.

Imagine Dragons was formed in 2008, and released their album Night Visions on September 4 2012. Smoke + Mirrors was released on Feburary 17 2015, and Evolve on June 23 2017. Their newest album Origins out on November 9 2018. New songs include Natural, Machine, Bad Liar, Digital, Zero, and Only.
